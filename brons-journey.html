Bron's Journey: I'll Take the Good News

About the book

With great personal courage, support of family and expert skills of staff at the Royal Prince Alfred Hospital, Bron recovered from leukemia and was eventually well enough to return to the outback. The nightmare resume when she was diagnosed with lymphoma.

Bron's Journey: I'll Take the Good News rings with the humorous incidents of outback life and offers inspiration for those times when we struggle with life's demands, especially when confronted with adverse health.
